Chicken Noodle Soup

What you'll need:

- onion
- garlic
- olive oil
- stock
- chicken
- pasta (optional)
- vegetables

What you'll do:

1. Dice onion and mince garlic.
2. Heat a saucepan to medium high-heat and add olive oil.
3. When oil starts to shimmer, add onion and garlic.
4. Cut vegetables into small pieces.
5. When onions start to become translucent, add vegetables.
6. When onions start to brown, add stock and cooked chicken.
   (You can add cooked pasta for a heartier soup.)
7. Once soup is boiling, lower heat to simmer and cover saucepan.
8. Cook soup until chicken and vegetables are tender.

Simple soup, comfy soup.
